Introducing the
"Tickle Tumble" Game!

Hey there, playful pals! Get ready to embark on a delightful journey into the world of tickles and giggles with our adorable day 10 project - "Tickle Tumble."

Imagine a world where feathers hold the power to tickle even the most stoic characters. In this charming game, you're invited to tickle our cute picture and make it giggle with joy! All you need is a simple click of your mouse or a tap on your screen, and watch the magic happen. As you click the feather, the picture will come to life, shaking and wiggling as if it's having the time of its life getting tickled.
